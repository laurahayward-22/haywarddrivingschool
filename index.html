About me

Following a 10 year career in Hospitality management, in 2018 I made the decision to become a driving instructor so that I could spend more time with my young family. I began training in October 2018 before qualifying as a DVSA Approved Driving Instructor (ADI) in July 2019.

I spent 3 years at a national driving school, where I gained valuable skills and experience on the roads of Edinburgh as an Instructor, before making the decision to venture out on my own 4 wheels and set up Hayward Driving School.

If you're looking for quality, no-nonsense, friendly, personable and calm driving instruction for both provisional and full licence holders in Edinburgh, then look no further.
